This function plots a matrix in a scrolling window with sliders 
that control the center of the plotted region and the zoom
ratio. 

Useage: plotmanipulator(matrix)

Submitted files include:
plotmanipulator.m & plotmanipulator.mat -- the graphic interface
zoomslider.m -- code to control actions taken by the sliders

Tested under Matlab 5.3
